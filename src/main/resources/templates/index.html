AGRA :- Indian City Of Monuments

During the Mughal era, Agra held significant importance as it served as the capital of their empire. In a bid to etch their names into history, each Mughal ruler undertook the construction of various monuments within Agra.

The following page features a compilation of the top historical monuments in Agra that are a must-visit. Regardless of the type of vacation you have in mind for Agra, these monuments promise to infuse your experience with a regal ambiance.
